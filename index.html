How it works

Choose, enter, customize, download.

  1. Pick a type. The form changes to match the QR format.
  2. Add content. The backend prepares the correct payload.
  3. Tune the look. Choose foreground, background, and export size.
  4. Download fast. Files are cached locally for the current app session.

FAQ

Practical answers.

Where are generated QR files stored?

FastAPI stores them in `output/session` for quick local downloads while the app runs.

What happens after closing the app?

The session folder is cleared on server shutdown and again on the next startup.

Do QR codes expire?

These are static QR codes. Once downloaded, the image keeps working as long as the encoded content remains valid.
